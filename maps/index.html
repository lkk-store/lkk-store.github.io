Backstory



When we went backpacking in high school, we would buy paper trail maps and wrap it in clear plastic and plan our trail on it with a Sharpie. When we hiked, we would place a compass on the map to navigate (we got lost a lot). I learned how to make maps when I grew up and I've always wanted to design and print my own trail maps. I recently saw a beautiful cloth map of the Everest trek in a friend's house and thought hey that's a good idea! I always see beautiful maps of places around the world but rarely see those of Hong Kong. This was the seed of this project.

中學嘅時候玩 AYP， 去地政署買紙本地圖，揾啲包書膠包住，plan 路程嘅時候攞 marker 上面畫條虛線，行山嗰陣擺個指南針喺張地圖上面扭嚟扭去（期間多次行錯路）。長大後嘅工作，學識咗自己整地圖，亦一直好想將自己喜歡嘅山徑設計地圖印製，但係嗡咗成世都未做呢件事。早排喺朋友屋企見到一塊好靚嘅布地圖，係佢去珠峰行山時買嘅記念品。我成日都見到世界各地好靚嘅地圖，但好少見到香港嘅山徑地圖，所以開始咗呢個計劃。
