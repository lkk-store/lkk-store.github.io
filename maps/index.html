香港四徑

HONG KONG FOUR TRAILS

香港有四條長途遠足徑，我將會喺 2022 年呢嘅暑假，完成呢 4 條山徑，作為自己一個小小嘅挑戰。人生好似不外乎諗啲目標俾自己然後向住嗰毎目標進發。

There are four long distance hiking trails in Hong Kong. I'm going to hike all of them this summer as a small personal challenge. Life seems like a coming up with goals for yourself and trying to accomplish them.
