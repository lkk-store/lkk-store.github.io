換言之香港街道是隨著1841年開埠而出現，直至現在全港已經超過四千條街道。

「水深港闊，山多平地少」是香港小學常識教科書對香港的描述，人人都耳熟能詳。

這個計劃以 「山多平地少」 作主題，分別選了香港七條街和道作為設計。


There were no streets and roads in Hong Kong before the city opened its first port in 1841. Now, Hong Kong has more than 4,000 streets and roads. The changes in streets and roads structure, their signs and their names reflect the story of this 180-year-old city.


The theme of this project is “hilly with little flat land”. The design features seven streets and seven roads in Hong Kong that illustrate the city's unique terrain.
